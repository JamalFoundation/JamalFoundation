Welcome to the Jamal Foundation

At Muzzafarabad Orphanage Organization, we are dedicated to providing love, care, and support to orphaned and vulnerable children in the heart of Pakistan. Situated in the picturesque region of Muzzafarabad, our organization is committed to offering a nurturing environment where children can flourish despite their challenging circumstances.

Established with a profound sense of compassion and a mission to make a difference, our orphanage stands as a beacon of hope for those in need. We believe that every child deserves a safe and loving home, regardless of their background or past experiences. Our team of dedicated professionals and volunteers work tirelessly to create a supportive community where children can heal, grow, and thrive.
